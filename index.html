EvacuMate

EvacuMate is a friendly Facebook messenger bot and map, built on a platform that changes how we think about managing natural disasters

It does this by:

  • Making community members aware of the possibility of a natural disaster and their responsibilities during an evacuation.
  • Helping disaster managers to better plan for and make smart choices during evacuations.
  • Giving the members of community informed options and the ability to establish a direct line of communication.

Unfortunately, I'm unable to give public access to EvacuMate because Facebook has a bot review policy that takes a number of days before bots are able to operate publicly. You can contact me @scriptsmith in the GovHack Slack, and I'm happy to add you to the list of people that can use it privately
